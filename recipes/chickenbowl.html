Chickenbowl

Indulge in the mouth-watering flavors of a delectable chicken bowl that's sure to leave your taste buds tingling with delight. This bowl features succulent chicken pieces perfectly cooked to tender perfection, paired with fluffy rice and a creamy tzatziki sauce that's both refreshing and savory. Topped with fresh slices of avocado, juicy tomatoes, and crisp cucumber, every bite is a burst of fresh, wholesome goodness that'll make you savor each and every morsel. Whether you're a fan of classic Mediterranean flavors or simply crave a healthy and satisfying meal, this chicken bowl is the perfect choice for a truly memorable dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 400g of Chicken Breast
  • 250g of Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Zaziki
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut Onions, Tomatoes, Avocados, Cucumber
  2. Wash and then marinate chicken breast
  3. Cook Onions
  4. Take out Onions and add Chicken Breast - high heat
  5. Add Rice and spices
  6. Add onions back in and let cook for couple minutes on low heat
  7. Put everything in a bowl, add Zaziki and enjoy
