Vegetable Soup

Experience the authentic taste of Greece with a mouthwatering moussaka that's sure to delight your taste buds. Layers of tender eggplant and savory ground beef are smothered in a rich tomato sauce, infused with fragrant herbs and spices. Topped with a creamy bechamel sauce and sprinkled with grated parmesan cheese, this dish is baked to golden perfection, creating a crispy and flavorful crust. Whether you're a fan of Mediterranean cuisine or simply looking for a hearty and satisfying meal, this Greek moussaka is the perfect choice. Each bite is a heavenly combination of creamy, cheesy, and meaty goodness that's impossible to resist. So sit back, relax, and let the flavors of Greece transport you to a world of culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • 400g of Potatoes
  • 3 Carrots
  • 1 Kohlrabi
  • 200g Peas
  • 200g Broccoli
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 500ml Vegetable Broth
  • 200ml Whipped Cream
  • Spices
  • Salt & Pepper
